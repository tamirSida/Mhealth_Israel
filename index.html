Building Israel's Innovation Ecosystem

mHealth Israel serves as a vital catalyst, connecting over 22,500 members from the technology and healthcare sectors. Founded in 2014 by digital health pioneer Levi Shapiro, our mission is to improve global health by fostering innovation and connecting Israeli startups with global partners, investors, and industry leaders.

HealthTech Focus

Digital Health, MedTech, AI in diagnostics, medical devices, clinical intelligence, genomics, and digital therapeutics.

ClimaTech Expansion
